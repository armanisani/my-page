About me

Arman Sani | Full Stack Web Developer

I’m a first generation Iranian American who recently decided to put aside my career in Sales & Network Marketing to pursue a future in full-stack Web Development. The world of coding is a world of constant learning and challenge that doesn’t prejudice where you come from, but only cares about where you’re going, and that’s what continues to fuel my passion to become a powerful web developer.

In my previous world of Network Marketing, I was required to grow myself into a leader quickly because I needed to lead a team of high performers to achieve their own successes & sales goals. I also realized that I quickly needed to become comfortable being uncomfortable if I ever wanted to see success, which meant I needed to be comfortable with public speaking in front of hundreds of people, comfortable cold marketing people to share the business opportunity/product, and comfortable getting used to hearing the most feared single word… “no”. Combine those experiences with my childhood where I moved around a lot due to my parents work and financial situation resulting in myself attending seven different schools. These situations blessed me with my avid learner personality, open mindedness, social skills, and a craving for solving challenges, that I plan to combine with my networking marketing skills and web development skills in my next role.

I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer with a focus on Ruby-on-Rails, MEAN stack, and Javascript. I enjoy both the front-end & back-end, but if I had to choose between one I would choose backend. In the future I hope to work with a company with a product I can stand by, a team I can work with, and a company that can take my skills from a junior level to a senior level.

Choose your own adventure game

Attempt to escape a mysterious room by picking your own path and dealing with the consequences of your action as your story unfolds. Play against a friend and see who can survive longer down the maze of paths! The objective of the game is to choose a path that you find best to take you onto the next stage. After you pick a path an event will pop up explaining the consequences of your decision. Not all paths will lead you forward some will kill your player, damage him greatly, or lead you in the wrong direction. You can find weapons and supplies that will help you along the way with paths. Each stage you continue onto will reward you with 5 points to your score, and if you hit certain milestones you will be rewarded more points. After your player 1 dies, player 2 will start from the beginning. Once both players die, the winner will be determined base off who has the most points. Built with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Bootstrap, jQuery
